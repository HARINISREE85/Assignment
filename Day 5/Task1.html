Tamilnadu

Tamil Nadu is a state in southern India. It is known for its ancient temples, beautiful beaches, and rich culture. Chennai is the capital city of Tamil Nadu.Tamil Nadu has a beautiful coastline along the Bay of Bengal and is home to popular tourist places like Ooty, Kodaikanal, Madurai, and Rameswaram. Tamil is the official language of the state and one of the oldest languages in the world. The people of Tamil Nadu are known for their hospitality and traditional festivals like Pongal.

Kerala

Kerala is a state in southern India. It is famous for its backwaters, green landscapes, spices, and beautiful beaches. Thiruvananthapuram is the capital city of Kerala.Kerala is famous for its traditional dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, as well as its classical music and colorful festivals such as Onam and Vishu. The state is also known for its high literacy rate and excellent healthcare system. People in Kerala are friendly and known for their hospitality.Tourism plays a big role in Kerala’s economy. Tourists from all over the world visit the state to experience its houseboats, spice gardens, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Karnataka

Karnataka is a state in southern India, known for its rich history, culture, and natural beauty. The capital city is Bengaluru, also called the Silicon Valley of India.The state is often called "The Land of Sandalwood" because of its famous sandalwood trees. Karnataka is also known for its coffee plantations and beautiful gardens.Karnataka has many historical sites such as Hampi, Mysore Palace, and Badami caves, which attract tourists from all over the world. Kannada is the official language of the state. People of Karnataka celebrate festivals like Dasara, Ugadi, and Karaga with great enthusiasm.

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a state in southern India. It is known for its rich culture, ancient temples, and beautiful landscapes. The capital city is Amaravati.The state has a long coastline along the Bay of Bengal and is famous for its beaches, such as Visakhapatnam and Bheemunipatnam.Andhra Pradesh is also known for its spicy cuisine, especially dishes like Andhra biryani, pulihora, and various chutneys.Telugu is the official language of the state. People of Andhra Pradesh celebrate festivals like Sankranti, Ugadi, and Dasara with great enthusiasm.

Delhi

Delhi is the capital city of India and a National Capital Territory. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and famous landmarks such as India Gate, Red Fort, and Qutub Minar.Delhi is a major political, economic, and cultural center in India. It hosts the Indian Parliament, the President’s residence, and several government offices. Delhi has excellent transport facilities, including the Delhi Metro, buses, and railway connections, making it well connected to all parts of India.
