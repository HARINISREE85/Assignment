Steps to Cook Fried Chicken🍗🐔🍖
  1. Clean and wash the chicken pieces properly.
  2. Marinate the chicken with buttermilk, salt, and pepper for at least 1 hour.
  3. Mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, and salt in a bowl for coating.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium flame.
  5. Coat the marinated chicken pieces with the flour mixture.
  6. Fry the chicken pieces in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove and drain excess oil using paper towels.
  8. Serve hot with sauce or your favorite dip.
