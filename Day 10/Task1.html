Apple is one of the most popular and loved fruits in the world. It is round in shape and comes in various colors like red, green, and yellow. Apples have a sweet or slightly sour taste depending on the type. They belong to the Rosaceae family, and their scientific name is Malus domestica. Apples grow on trees that are mainly found in cool and temperate regions. The fruit has smooth skin and a juicy inside, with small brown seeds in the center. Apples are rich in vitamins and minerals, especially Vitamin C and dietary fiber, which help boost immunity and support digestion. The saying “An apple a day keeps the doctor away” reminds us of its great health benefits. Apples are low in calories, making them a healthy snack for everyone.
