Different margin values and compare spacing

margin property creates spacing around an HTML element, outside of its border. It defines the empty space between an element and its adjacent elements or the edge of the containing element/viewport.

The CSS margin property creates space around elements. Margins define the outer space of elements, helping to separate and position them effectively on a web page.

Margins are distinct from padding, which creates space inside an element, between its content and its border.
